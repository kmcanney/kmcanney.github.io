Channel Volume

Choose Effects > Sony Creative Software > Channel Volume to change the volume of a selection.

You can also use the Normalize dialog to maximize the volume of a sound file. For more information, see "Normalizing audio."

  1. Select the data you want to modify. For more information, see "Selecting data using the mouse."

  2. If no data is selected, the volume of the entire file will be modified.

  3. Choose Effects > Sony Creative Software > Channel Volume.

  4. Drag the Master fader to adjust the volume of the selection, or you can drag a channel fader to adjust channel levels independently.

  5. Click the Process button.

Inserting silence

Choose Insert > Silence to insert a section of silence in a sound file.

  1. Choose Edit > Mode > Time (or click the Time Mode button in an editor's playbar).

  2. Choose Insert > Silence. The Insert Silence dialog is displayed.

  3. In the Insert box, specify the length of silence that you want to add.

  4. Choose a setting from the at drop-down list to specify where the silence should be inserted:

    • Cursor: inserts silence at the current cursor position.

    • Start of file: inserts silence at the beginning of the file.

    • End of file: inserts silence at the end of the file.

  5. Click the Insert button.

    Any markers or regions that exist after the insertion point will be rippled to preserve their position.

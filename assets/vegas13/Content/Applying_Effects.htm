Applying effects

You can use effects to manipulate the audio and video components of your project.

The included audio plug-ins can improve the quality of the audio in your project or create unique sounds. VegasÂ® Pro supports additional DirectX and VST effects from a variety of vendors. Audio effects can be applied to all events on a track, to busses, or as assignable effects.

The included video plug-ins are comprised of effects, transitions, and text/background generators. Effects can be used to improve video quality or manipulate an image, transitions can change the way video events flow into one another, and generators can create text and colored backgrounds.

Tips:

  • To add effects quickly, you can drag plug-ins and plug-in packages from the Plug-In Manager window to an audio track header. For more information, see "Creating and using effects packages" and "The Plug-In Manager Window."

  • Use the VST Effects tab in the Preferences dialog to indicate where the application should search for VST plug-ins and to select which plug-ins you want to use.For more information, see "Preferences - VST Effects."

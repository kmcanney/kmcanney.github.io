Getting Video Off of or Onto Your Camcorder

With Vegas Pro, you can capture video from DV cameras, DVD cameras, HDV cameras, and SDI cards or print to tape using a DV device or deck connected to an SDI card.

  • For information about capturing DV clips, see "Capturing video."

  • For information about importing video from a DVD camcorder, see "Importing video from a DVD camcorder."

  • For information about capturing HDV clips, see "Capturing HDV clips."

  • For information about capturing clips from a deck connected to an SDI card, see "Capturing from an SDI card."

  • For information about importing clips from a RED camera, see "Working with RED camera clips."

  • For information about importing clips from an AVCHD camera, see "Working with AVCHD video."

  • For information about importing clips from an XDCAM camera or deck, see "XDCAM and XDCAM HD workflow."

  • For information about importing clips from an XDCAM EX camera or deck, see "XDCAM EX workflow."

  • For information about printing to tape, see "Printing video to tape."

  • For more information about using IEEE-1394 devices, see http://www.sonycreativesoftware.com/Support/Productinfo/OHCI.asp.

  • More information about OHCI-compliant IEEE-1394DV devices can also be found in our knowledge base: http://www.custcenter.com/.

Creating Slow-Motion Video

If you want to create a slow-motion video effect, Vegas Pro provides two methods you can use:

  • Hold Ctrl while dragging the edge of the event to time-stretch the event. For more information, see "Adjusting an event's length."

  • Right-click a video event, choose Insert/Remove Envelope from the shortcut menu, and then choose Velocity from the submenu to add a velocity envelope to the event. For more information, see "Event envelopes."

When video is slowed down, its frame rate will be lower than the project frame rate, and Vegas Pro will resample the video to produce smooth motion. To ensure video is resampled as needed, set the Resample event switch to Force Resample or Smart Resample. For more information, see "Applying switches to events."

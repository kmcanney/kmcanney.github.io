The Vegas Pro Window

The VegasÂ® Pro window is where you edit your project, and the screen is divided into several areas.

Tips:

  • If you prefer to work with the timeline at the bottom of the window and the docking area at the top of the window, select the Display timeline at bottom of main window check box on the Display tab of the Preferences dialog. For more information, see "Preferences - Display Tab."

  • The track list, timeline, and window docking area sections can be sized to your preferences by dragging the dividers between them or by using F11:

    • F11 minimizes and restores the window docking area.

    • Shift+F11 minimizes and restores the track list.

    • Ctrl+F11 maximizes and restores the timeline vertically and horizontally (window docking area and track list will be hidden).

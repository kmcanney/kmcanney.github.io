Using Vegas Pro Connect

Vegas Pro Connect makes it easier for you to edit and collaborate on Vegas Pro projects.

When you're working with Vegas Pro, you can use Vegas Pro Connect to remotely control the Vegas transport functions, seek and scrub the timeline, and add markers.

When you're on the go, you can use Vegas Pro Connect to bring your projects with you for review on your mobile device.

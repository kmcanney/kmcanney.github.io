Mixing audio

You can mix your audio tracks in three ways in VegasÂ® Pro:

  • The audio track controls in the track list allow you to adjust relative track volumes, panning, assignable FX send levels, and bus send levels for individual tracks.

  • The Master Bus window allows you to adjust the overall level of your project.

  • The Mixing Console window allows for advanced multichannel mixing using multiple busses and assignable effects using a traditional hardware-style mixer view.

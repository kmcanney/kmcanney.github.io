The Video FX Window

Choose View > Window > Video FX to toggle the display of the Video FX window.

The left pane lists each of the available video effects organized in folders. Click the Expand and Collapse buttons to open and close the folders, and select a plug-in name. The thumbnail images in the right pane represent each of the existing presets for the selected effect. Hover your cursor over a preset to see an animated example.

You can type in the Search plug-ins box to find plug-ins. For example, if you wanted to find a specific color-correction plug-in, you could type "color" in the box to display only plug-ins that include the term "color" in the plug-in name, description, or group name.

You can drag a preset thumbnail to a track, event, or to the Video Preview window to apply the effect.

For more information, see "Adding video effects."
